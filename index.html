About Us

Nasoca is a non-profit organization run by three high schoolers—Natalie, Sophia, and Catalina—who are dedicated to making a positive impact in their community. With a deep passion for giving back, they founded Nasoca to serve those in need and to foster a spirit of kindness and generosity. Their commitment to the organization and its mission drives them to continuously seek out new ways to support their community, inspire others to get involved, and create lasting change. Through Nasoca, they aim to build a network of compassionate individuals who work together to make the world a better place, one act of kindness at a time.
